A grand free medical camp was held on 17/09/2021 on the occasion of the 71st birthday of the Hon'ble Prime Minister of India in collaboration with Star Blind Welfare Trust, Harshini Multi Speciality Hospital and Samayanallur Government Primary Health Center at Anna Community Hall, Oormachikulam, Madurai District.

In the camp held, 93 beneficiaries got ear, nose and throat examination free of charge and 12 people benefitted by getting hearing aids under the program. 22 people were given 1st dose covishield vaccine & 5 people were given 2nd dose covishield vaccine, 132 beneficiaries participated in the camp and benefited.

At the function held, Star blind welfare trust chaiman K.Mohan and in the presence of All India Shaksam President Mr. Govindaraj, Pandyan Rotary Club Past Present Mr. Sivakumar Madurai Pandyan Rotary Club Past Present Chairman Nachiyar gave a keynote speech in the ceremony along with Bharatnatyam and other events.
